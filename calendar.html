Club Meeting and Observing Calendar

The club conducts regular monthly indoor meetings. Currently, we meet Fridays for a business/discussion meeting followed, weather permitting, by an observing session. For 2016, we will hold these meetings in the Student Center at KSC in Keene, NH, club members homes, or at the Sullivan Observatory depending on the season.

For 2016, we plan on having an informative astronomy-related presentation immediately following each business meeting. We also hold monthly scheduled observing sessions at our Sullivan Observatory. Our 2016 schedule is as follows:
