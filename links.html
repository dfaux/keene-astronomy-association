  • Sky Charts
    • Abram's Sky Calendar - sheet for each month takes the form of a calendar, the reverse contains a star map of the month's evening sky, available by subscription and received quarterly through the mail
    • AstroViewer - AstroViewer is an interactive night sky map that helps you to find your way in the night sky quickly and easily.
    • The Constellations and their Stars - A great overview of the constellations
    • The Dome of the Sky - Constellations, Mythology, Stars, and interactive maps for locations around the world
    • MyStarsLive - generates horizon view sky maps for sites all over the world
    • Deep Sky Observing - Exploring a world of clusters, galaxies, nebulae, supernova remnants, etc.
    • Que Tal? in the Current Sky - an Earth in Space Monthly Newsletter
    • SkyGlobe Download - one of many sites from which the SkyGlobe Planetarium Shareware Program can be downloaded, it is an excellent program in my opinion (runs on PCs only)
    • Sky Chart for Keene, NH - courtesy of the Heavens Above Satellite Prediction Page, VERY easy to use!
    • Skymaps.com - 2-page monthly guide to the night skies of the northern and southern hemispheres including a sky chart and a short descriptive article
    • StarCalc - planetarium and star mapping shareware program for PCs (Windows 9x/ME/NT/2000/XP) and PocketPCs
    • Uncle Al's Sky Wheels - make-it-yourself planisphere (constellation map good for any time of night, any night of the year - I believe it is for ~40° N latitude)
    • Weather Underground Sky: Keene, NH - sky map for Keene, NH (lat = ~ 42° N); date, time, and direction can be easily changed
    • Weather Underground Astronomy - get a map specifically for your location by entering the zipcode of your observing site here
    • Wikisky - The NEWS@SKY (Space&Science) is a unique opportunity to browse hundreds of scientific and popular science Internet site
